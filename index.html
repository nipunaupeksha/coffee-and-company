Welcome to Coffee and Company!

Coffee and Company is the vision of Marie-Eve Laroche, who on discovering the coffee culture of Sri Lanka saw an opportunity to enrich the Colombo landscape. Working with Canadian roasters Phil & Sebastian and Microtorrefacteur Saint-Henri, the drinks at Coffee and Company are crafted with the goal always to showcase the intrinsic character of individual coffees and served with casual familiarity. Paired with locally homemade recipes from Hof Kelsten and Godley & Creme, the cafe offers everything needed for a quick stop on the way to work or a catch-up with an old friend.
